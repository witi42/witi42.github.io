Clipmate AI

Main developer of Clipmate, an AI bookmark manager.

Together with my co-founder Moritz, I’ve been working on Clipmate for half a year. Our main motivation was the struggle to find what we were looking for among thousands of Twitter bookmarks, web links, and screenshots. We started with an iOS app but are now focussing on a web app that syncs and organizes all of your content.
